## âš¡ Upgrades

- **Luck Upgrade**: Increases merge success rate (75% base + up to 50% bonus)
- **Income Multiplier**: Boosts all pet income (up to 2x at max level)
- **Click Power**: Adds bonus money per click (up to +$40 at max level)
- **Auto Clicker**: Automatically clicks for you (up to 50 clicks/sec)
- **Critical Clicks**: Chance for 5x click earnings (up to 6% crit chance)
- **Egg Discount**: Reduces egg costs (up to 40% discount)
- **Pet Value Boost**: Increases pet income rates (up to 96% bonus)
- **Multi-Hatch**: Hatch multiple eggs at once (up to 6 eggs simultaneously)

Each upgrade has increasing costs and maximum levels to prevent infinite grinding.

## ğŸ¯ How to Play
